Why FileCourier?

Everything you need, nothing you don't. No account, no cloud, no limits — just a link and a transfer.

🔒
Zero Server Storage

Your files are never uploaded anywhere. Data streams peer-to-peer, device to device, leaving zero trace on any server.

🌎
Works Everywhere

Home WiFi, different ISPs, mobile data — FileCourier auto-adapts. STUN for direct connections, TURN relay as fallback.

📊
No File Size Limit

Chrome and Edge stream files directly to disk — no RAM limit, no waiting. Other browsers handle up to 512 MB.

🔐
End-to-End Encrypted

Every connection is protected by DTLS-SRTP — the same encryption standard used in secure banking and video calls.

⭐
Free Forever

No subscriptions, no credits, no paywalls. Open source under MIT license. Self-host it yourself if you want.

📤
Send Up to 10 Files

Queue up to 10 files at once. Each is offered individually — receiver accepts or declines before bytes flow.

How it works

Three steps, no account, no install. Works in any modern browser.

1
📤
Open FileCourier

A unique share link is generated instantly on your device. No login, no setup.

2
🔗
Share the link

Copy the link and send it via WhatsApp, email, SMS — anything. Add up to 10 files to the queue.

3
⚡
File transfers

Your friend opens the link, accepts the file, and it streams directly from your device — encrypted, instant.
